Deazy, the venue recommendation app that weighs data from Google and FourSquare to find the best venue near you that fits your mood.

With Deazy, Dates are made Eazy

Simply select your search settings:

  1. Pick a category!
    • What are you in the mood for? Select from a range of venue categories:
      • Cocktails
      • Dessert
      • Dinner
      • Live Music
      • Dance Clubs
      • Karaoke
  2. How much are you willing to spend!
    • Filter venues by how expensive the venue has been rated as
    • Venues outside of the selected price tiers will be excluded
  3. Select a minimum rating!
    • Venues rated below the selected minimum rating will be excluded
  4. Determine how far you're willing to commute!
    • Venues outside the selected range will be filtered out
  5. Set your preferences
    • Decide what is most important to you
    • Would you rather travel a bit further to go to a higher rated venue or stay close to home?
    • Maybe you just want to find the cheapest place near you
    • This is where you make that choice
